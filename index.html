About Me

Welcome to my corner of the web! I'm Purna B. Pradhan, a passionate web developer, freelancer, computer science teacher, and social media manager. With a versatile skill set and a dedication to excellence, I strive to bring creativity, innovation, and efficiency to every project I undertake.

  • Web Developer: As a web developer, I specialize in creating dynamic, user-friendly websites that not only look great but also perform seamlessly. My expertise spans front-end and back-end development, ensuring a holistic approach to web solutions.
  • Freelancer: In my freelance work, I collaborate with clients from diverse industries, providing tailored solutions that meet their unique needs. Whether it's building a brand-new site or enhancing an existing one, my goal is to deliver exceptional results that drive success.
  • Computer Science Teacher: Sharing knowledge is a passion of mine. As a computer science teacher, I have the privilege of educating the next generation of tech enthusiasts. My teaching approach is hands-on and engaging, helping students understand complex concepts and develop practical skills.
  • Social Media Manager: In today's digital age, a strong social media presence is crucial. I help businesses and individuals build and maintain a compelling online presence, creating content and strategies that resonate with their audience and amplify their reach.
I invite you to explore my portfolio and get in touch to discuss how we can work together to achieve your digital goals. Let's create something amazing!
